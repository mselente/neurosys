An enterprise learning platform used by 40,000+ learners worldwide. With Dify-powered AI built into the product itself, Samelane is taking the natural step from traditional LMS to Competency Intelligence.

The product

Samelane is an enterprise learning platform for training, performance, compliance and engagement - serving 40,000+ learners globally for organizations like Comcast, Grupa Azoty, Science Pharma, Medivet and many more. EU-based with offices across the UK, Poland and Norway, the team has been operating in the LMS market since 2017.

  • Course Catalogue - the full library of available courses across the organization
  • Course Builder - create courses from scratch or reuse PDFs, video, audio, HTML5, articles, images and homeworks
  • SCORM & xAPI uploader - latest compliant SCORM cloud and Tin Can xAPI standards for content interoperability
  • Instructor-led training & webinars - set up, recorded and maintained directly in the platform
  • Calendars, scheduling & reminders - automated alerts that keep learners on track
  • Quizzes & assessments - knowledge checks, exams and skill validation tied to learning tracks

From LMS to Competency Intelligence

The L&D market is changing fast. Companies expect their learning platform to do more than host courses: it should recommend the right learning, generate content on demand, surface insights about competencies and help managers act on them. Samelane is taking that step - moving from a traditional LMS to a Competency Intelligence platform, with AI built into the product surface itself.

Where AI shows up inside Samelane

Instead of bolting a chatbot beside the platform, Samelane uses Dify-powered agentic workflows as native product capability. The AI moments live inside the flows trainers and learners already know:

  • Content & course generation - turn raw materials, PDFs and notes into structured course modules with quizzes, drafts and learning paths
  • Report summaries - turn long activity, completion and compliance reports into clear, decision-ready summaries for L&D leads and HR
  • Semantic search - learners and admins find the right course, lesson or topic by meaning across the entire content library

Why it matters

The Samelane team brings deep IT and AI experience, years of running L&D processes for enterprise clients, and a clear understanding of HR-tech challenges. AI inside the product is an operational layer that changes how trainers build, how learners discover, and how leaders act on the data they already have.

  • Faster time to first published course
  • Less manual work for L&D teams maintaining content
  • Better discovery and engagement for learners
  • Insight into actual competencies and skill levels

Trusted by enterprise teams

Comcast, Grupa Azoty, Car Net, Science Pharma, USP Zdrowie, Access Healthcare Physicians, Jump Digital and Medivet are among the organizations using Samelane to run learning at scale.

The bottom line

An LMS that ships AI as a native product feature, native to the learning experience. The natural evolution: LMS → Competency Intelligence.
